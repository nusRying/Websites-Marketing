# Project: Automated Website Sales Engine

## Core Value
Building an automated sales engine that identifies businesses without websites and sells them a custom-built solution through highly personalized AI-driven outreach featuring live, personalized "Sample Websites".

## Constraints & Principles
- **Show, Don't Just Tell:** Every lead gets a live sample site.
- **Deliverability First:** Protect domain reputation at all costs.
- **Scalable Automation:** Minimize manual data cleaning and enrichment.
- **Distributed Architecture:** Separate ingestion, display, and outreach.

## Tech Stack (Planned)
- **Lead Sourcing:** Apollo.io
- **Enrichment:** Clay
- **Website Gen:** Next.js (App Router), Vercel, Supabase
- **Outreach:** Smartlead.ai / Instantly.ai
- **Verification:** MillionVerifier
- **AI:** GPT-4o
- **Automation:** Puppeteer (Screenshots)
