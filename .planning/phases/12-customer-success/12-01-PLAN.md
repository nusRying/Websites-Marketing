# Phase 12: Customer Success & Churn Mitigation Plan

<objective>
Maximize Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) by identifying health risks early and creating personalized pathways for long-term user advocacy.
</objective>

<tasks>

### Task 1: Health Score Analytics
- **Usage Tracking**: Log "Critical Events" (Scrape Started, Outreach CSV Exported, Meeting Booked) to a global analytics dashboard (using Mixpanel or PostHog).
- **Churn Prediction**: Identify "At-Risk" users (e.g., users who haven't run a scrape in 14 days) and trigger a personalized "Strategy Check-in" email.

### Task 2: Personalized Role-Based Paths
- **Role Selection**: During onboarding, ask if the user is a "Solo Founder", "Sales Rep", or "Agency Owner".
- **Customized Dashboard**: Show different primary actions based on the role (e.g., Agency Owners see higher-level batch volume stats; Sales Reps see 'HOT' lead alerts).

### Task 3: Churn Salvage Flow
- **Exit Survey**: Implement a multi-choice survey when a user clicks 'Cancel' (Price too high, Too complex, Not enough leads, etc.).
- **Dynamic Salvage**:
  - Price too high -> Offer 20% discount for 3 months.
  - No leads -> Offer 100 free 'Gold' credits to try a different niche.
  - Too complex -> Offer a free 1-on-1 onboarding session.

</tasks>

<success_criteria>
- Month-over-month churn rate is under 5%.
- Active Advocacy: Identify 5 power users for case studies within the first 90 days.
</success_criteria>
